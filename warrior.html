The warrior spirit in art

Portrait of a Warrior

Japanese Samurai

By Koketso Sekete

The artist captures both the military might and the refined aesthetic sensibility characteristic of the period.

Notice the intricate details of the armor's lacing and the expressive rendering of the facial features, which convey the subject's formidable character.

  • A detailed face mask
  • Chest plate with decorative etching
  • Shoulder guards
  • Arm covers

This portrait was commissioned shortly before the knight's most famous battle. The artwork reflects the transition from medieval to Renaissance art techniques.
